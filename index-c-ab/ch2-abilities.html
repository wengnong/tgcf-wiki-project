Abilities

  • Ghost: As a ghost, Hua Cheng doesn't age and has no ned for necessities like food or sleep. He is also unable to die unless his ashes were to be destroyed, or he left the world on his own accord.

  • Devastation Rank: Since he is of Devastation rank, he possesses enough power to wipe out an entire nation and throw the mortal realm into chaos.

  • Spiritual Powers: He has an abundance of spiritual powers.

  • Blood Rain: One of his most notorious powers is summoning blood rain at will. The rain doesn't appear to be harmful and behaves like ordinary rain that is coloured red.

  • Weapon Gathering: He has a large collection of rare antiques and powerful weapons.

  • Sculpture Craftsmanship: He is extremely skilled with sculpting statues, as evidenced by the numerous lifelike status of Xie Lian in the Ten Thousand Gods Cave.

  • Painting: He has decent skills in painting, shown when he painted the portrait of Xie Lian in Puqi Shrine.

  • Wealth: He is one of the wealthiest characters.

  • Domestic Skills: He displays good expertise in doing household chores and labour such as building doors.

  • Luck

Weapons

  • Wraith Butterflies: In Hua Cheng's control, the butterflies become quite a versatile tool. They are capable of great offense as a swarm of them can take down many powerful enemies and also work as a nigh-impenetrable defense. These butterflies can also do recon work such as scouting an area, spying on targets, turning invisible, record & edit and also work as communication devices. Some butterflies even have the capability to heal wounds.

  • E-Ming: Hua Cheng's sentient scimitar that he often treats like a disobedient dog. This weapon was famous for being incredibly wicked, with an oppressive sense of evil and malice. One slash from E-Ming and his enemy will be poisoned to death. In the case that they survive, Hua Cheng can order the healed wound to open again at his will and kill his enemy from a distance. A truly terrifying weapon.

  • Distance Shortening Array Dice: He has a pair of dice that when rolled allows him to transport to different locations while predicting danger ahead.
